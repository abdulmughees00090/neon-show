Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

Neon Dream is an interactive art experience. We collect minimal, anonymized analytics data (such as session duration and general interaction metrics) to understand how the dream is experienced. We do not require you to create an account.

2. Payment Information

All financial transactions are securely processed by our Merchant of Record, Paddle. We do not store or process your credit card information or payment details directly. Please refer to Paddle's privacy policy for details on how they handle your payment data.

3. Use of Information

The minimal data we collect is used solely to maintain, improve, and understand the artistic experience of Neon Dream.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at info@silverfoxdynamics.com.
